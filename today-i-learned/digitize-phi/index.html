Digitize Phi

Exploring the decimal digits of φ (1.6180339887...)

Welcome to the Digitize Phi project! This page documents a year-long exploration into the decimal digits of the golden ratio, φ. The project, which began on June 26, 2025, and will conclude on June 26, 2026, reveals 10,000 new decimal digits of φ each day. The image below, titled “Daily Histogram,” is a static visualization of the frequency of the digits 0 through 9 for today's 10,000 digits. To see visualizations from previous days, please visit the link below titled “Histogram Archive.” Further down, the “Dynamic Histogram” is a cumulative and interactive chart displaying the frequency of all digits collected since the project's inception. This allows you to see how the distribution of digits is evolving over time.
